Sanne van Boheemen

About

Entrepreneur 3D printing technician & designer turned Web Developer. a dynamic and resourceful professional with a passion for creativity and problem-solving. As a self-employed 3D print technician and designer, Sanne has honed his skills in bringing innovative ideas to life.

With over a decade of experience as volunteer event organizer, Sanne thrived in leading teams of volunteers, handling logistics and and organized events that left a lasting impact on participants. His remarkable ability to handle challenges with a keen eye for detail and unique problem-solving solutions sets them apart.

Sanne's path to success has been shaped by personal growth and resilience. Overcoming clinical depression and embracing their diagnosis of ADD and High-Functioning Autism Spectrum Disorder, they discovered their innate talent for hands-on learning and a passion for understanding how things work. This led to a rewarding career as a product designer, developing tools for Visually Impaired Persons (VIPs) and establishing their own company, H3X Designs, where they empower others by transforming ideas into reality through 3D printing.

Driven to continuously evolve, Sanne is now pursuing new opportunities in Sweden, broadening their skill set with web development through online courses like Code the Dream. Their dedication to learning, combined with their exceptional reasoning and analytical abilities, promises an exciting future filled with innovation and positive impact.
